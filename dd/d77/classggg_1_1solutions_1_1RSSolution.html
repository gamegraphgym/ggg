Detailed Description

template<typename GraphType, typename StrategyType = ggg::strategy::DeterministicStrategy<GraphType>>
class ggg::solutions::RSSolution< GraphType, StrategyType >

Convenience solution combining Regions (R) and Strategies (S).

Capability: R + S

RSSolution aggregates RSolution and SSolution to provide both winning-region information and positional strategies in a single type suitable as a solver return.
