using ggg::parity::graph::StandardValidator = typedef graphs::CompositeValidator< Graph, PlayerValidator<0, 1>, PriorityValidator<0>, OutDegreeValidator<1>, NoDuplicateEdgesValidator>

Standard composite validator for 2-player turn-based parity games.

This validator checks:

  • Players are either 0 or 1
  • Priorities are non-negative
  • All vertices have at least one outgoing edge
  • No duplicate edges exist
